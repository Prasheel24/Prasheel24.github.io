About me

I am a graduate student in Robotics at the University of Maryland College Park. I am interested in the field of Robot Decision Making and Controls. I am actively seeking internship opportunities for Summer 2020.

I am currently working under Prof. Huan Xu at the X-Lab to simulate a fixed wing UAS for mission planning with onboard situational awareness and re-planning. We have deployed and tested the first phase of the project on an aircraft using PixHawk (GC). Now, I am working towards the next phase of the project, to build code for Obstacle Avoidance in ROS for the system.

In my free time, I love to read mystery novels & go swimming. I was the campus ambassador for Webo (NGO) during my undergrad & an active volunteer for CSR events held in Accenture. In this semester (Spring 2020), I am a Student Volunteer at the UMD Data Challenge 2020.
